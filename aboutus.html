ABOUT US

Maharajadhiraj Prithvi Narayan Shah (1723–1775) (Nepali: श्री ५ बडामहाराजाधिराज पृथ्वीनारायण शाह देव) was the last ruler of the Gorkha Kingdom and first monarch of the Kingdom of Nepal (also called Kingdom of Gorkha). Prithvi Narayan Shah started the unification of Nepal.Shah proclaimed the newly unified Kingdom of Nepal as Asal Hindustan ("Real Land of Hindus") due to North India being ruled by the Islamic Mughal rulers. He also referred to the rest of Northern India as Mughlan (Country of Mughals)Prithvi Narayan Shah ismap considered as the Father of the Nation in Nepal. This is our inspiration.

Now, a little bit about our fashion styles!! Unlike the classic style, the modern fashion style embraces bold looks, statement pieces, and loud bright colors. As the trends are constantly changing every couple of years, it could be a bit less budget-friendly to stick to this way of dressing than sticking to the elegant basics. How has pop influenced fashion? Nowadays, what we see online also significantly influences our personal styles. Celebrities and influencers command pop culture fashion trends by posting their own attire and favorite brands on social media platforms. These platforms have a huge global reach and massive followings of younger trendsetters in the making.
